## Making a Payment

### Step-by-Step Payment Process

<Card padding="24" radius="l" border="neutral-alpha-medium" marginBottom="24">
  <Column gap="20">
    <Row gap="12" horizontal="start" vertical="center">
      <Text variant="heading-strong-xl" onBackground="brand-medium">1</Text>
      <Column gap="8" fillWidth>
        <Text variant="heading-strong-m" onBackground="neutral-strong">Access Your Invoice</Text>
        <Text variant="body-default-s" onBackground="neutral-weak">
          Log in to your customer account in WHMCS, navigate to "Invoices", find the unpaid invoice, and click "Pay Now"
        </Text>
      </Column>
    </Row>
    
    <Row gap="12" horizontal="start" vertical="center">
      <Text variant="heading-strong-xl" onBackground="brand-medium">2</Text>
      <Column gap="8" fillWidth>
        <Text variant="heading-strong-m" onBackground="neutral-strong">Select Payment Method</Text>
        <Text variant="body-default-s" onBackground="neutral-weak">
          You'll see available payment options. Select "PhonePe Payment Gateway" and click "Continue"
        </Text>
      </Column>
    </Row>
    
    <Row gap="12" horizontal="start" vertical="center">
      <Text variant="heading-strong-xl" onBackground="brand-medium">3</Text>
      <Column gap="8" fillWidth>
        <Text variant="heading-strong-m" onBackground="neutral-strong">Choose Payment Method</Text>
        <Text variant="body-default-s" onBackground="neutral-weak">
          Select your preferred payment method: UPI, Net Banking, Debit/Credit Card, or Digital Wallet
        </Text>
      </Column>
    </Row>
    
    <Row gap="12" horizontal="start" vertical="center">
      <Text variant="heading-strong-xl" onBackground="brand-medium">4</Text>
      <Column gap="8" fillWidth>
        <Text variant="heading-strong-m" onBackground="neutral-strong">Complete Transaction</Text>
        <Text variant="body-default-s" onBackground="neutral-weak">
          Follow PhonePe's payment process, enter OTP if required, confirm payment amount, and submit payment
        </Text>
      </Column>
    </Row>
    
    <Row gap="12" horizontal="start" vertical="center">
      <Text variant="heading-strong-xl" onBackground="brand-medium">5</Text>
      <Column gap="8" fillWidth>
        <Text variant="heading-strong-m" onBackground="neutral-strong">Payment Confirmation</Text>
        <Text variant="body-default-s" onBackground="neutral-weak">
          You'll receive payment confirmation, be redirected back to WHMCS, receipt generated, and confirmation email sent
        </Text>
      </Column>
    </Row>
  </Column>
</Card>

## Payment Confirmation

### Receipt

<Text variant="body-default-m" onBackground="neutral-weak" marginBottom="12">
You receive a receipt after payment containing:
</Text>

- Transaction reference number
- Amount paid
- Payment date and time
- Invoice number
- Merchant details
- Payment method used

### Where to Find

- Email inbox (confirmation email)
- WHMCS account under invoices
- PhonePe transaction history
