## Using the App

- **Dashboard:** Recent invoices, services, tickets
- **Services:** View/manage hosting products
- **Invoices:** Pay and download invoices
- **Support:** Open and track tickets
- **Offline mode:** Recent invoices, tickets, services available when offline; syncs on reconnect

## Troubleshooting

- **Cannot find install option:** Ensure HTTPS; use Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS); refresh page
- **App not loading:** Check connection, clear app cache, reinstall
- **Cannot log in:** Verify credentials; reset password; clear cache/cookies
