# PWA Plus for WHMCS

<Text variant="body-default-xl" onBackground="neutral-weak" marginBottom="32">
  Transform your WHMCS Client Area and Admin Area into fully installable, cross-platform Progressive Web Apps—**without publishing to app stores, without coding, and without any changes to the WHMCS core**.
</Text>

## Compatibility

<Column fillWidth gap="12" marginTop="24" marginBottom="40">
  <Row gap="8" horizontal="start" vertical="center">
    <Icon name="checkCircle" size="s" onBackground="brand-medium" />
    <Text variant="body-default-s" onBackground="neutral-weak">Compatible with **WHMCS 8.0 and later**</Text>
  </Row>
  <Row gap="8" horizontal="start" vertical="center">
    <Icon name="checkCircle" size="s" onBackground="brand-medium" />
    <Text variant="body-default-s" onBackground="neutral-weak">Works with **cPanel, Plesk, DirectAdmin**, and custom servers</Text>
  </Row>
  <Row gap="8" horizontal="start" vertical="center">
    <Icon name="checkCircle" size="s" onBackground="brand-medium" />
    <Text variant="body-default-s" onBackground="neutral-weak">Supports **PHP 7.4** and **PHP 8.x**</Text>

    <Icon name="checkCircle" size="s" onBackground="brand-medium" />
