## Support Categories

- **Sales:** sales@sapphive.com — demos, licensing, pricing
- **Technical:** support@sapphive.com — install, configuration, bugs
- **Migrations:** migrations@sapphive.com — migration and upgrades
- **Compliance:** compliance@sapphive.com — GDPR, security, policy
- **Abuse:** abuse@sapphive.com — violations and reports

## What to Include

- WHMCS version and PHP version
- Server type (cPanel, Plesk, DirectAdmin, custom)
- PWA Plus version
- Steps to reproduce
- Expected vs actual behavior
- Screenshots or console errors
